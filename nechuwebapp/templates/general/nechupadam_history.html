How Nechupadam Dental Clinic

Was Founded

1965-1991

1965

Nechupadam Dental Clinic was established on August 17th by Dr K T Paulose, who was a Gold Medal Student of Govt Dental College Trivandrum for all 4 years of Dental Education.

1971

Nechupadam Dental Clinic was started at Perumbavoor after Dr Paulose took over the practice of veteran Dentist Dr Balan. The clinic was later sold to JJ dental clinic group in 1976.

1981

Nechupadam Dental Clinic equipped with X-rays and specialists including Crown and Bridge and oral rehabilitation team.

1990

Free Dental clinic on Friday afternoon was inaugurated by Dr K R Rajan IAS District Collector Ernakulam as part of Silver Jubilee Celebrations to serve the needy.

1969

Dental Wing was established at MOSC Medical Mission Hospital Kolenchery with Dr K T Paulose Nechupadam as the chief Dental Surgeon.

1976

Nechupadam Dental Clinic became the first full-fledged private Orthodontic practice in Kerala, equipped with a team of dentists and lab technicians, and an in-house lab for dental prosthesis and orthodontic appliances under the leadership of Orthodontist Dr Paulose K T, who secured Masters in Orthodontics from Govt Dental College Trivandrum.

1985

The clinic became fully air-conditioned and implemented sterilization and disinfection protocols.

1991

Dr Nechupadam Dental Clinic @ Marine Drive GCDA Complex was fully equipped with all modern equipment, including dental X-rays and a minor prosthetic and orthodontic lab.

2000-2015

2000

Dr Thomas Nechupadam joined Dr Nechupadam Dental Clinic and revamped it with digital X-rays and single visit rotary endodontic treatment.

2002

Dr. Sajil John, a Periodontist, joins the oral restorative team, strengthening the clinic’s capabilities.

2008

Dr. Nithia Sara Thomas joins the Nechupadam Dental team.

2014

Nechupadam Dental is recognized as an international pageant groomer for International Fashion Fest and national pageants.

2001

Nechupadam Dental Implantology and implant prosthetic clinic is established.

2005

Nechupadam Dental becomes the first internationally certified dental clinic in Kerala, with a focus on dental tourism as a key area. The clinic also launches the Nechupadam Dental Portal and live TV shows with Malayalam TV channels.

2010

Nechupadam Dental starts Youtube channels for dental education and patient awareness, including “God’s Own Dentist“ and “Keraladentaltours.“

2015

The clinic celebrates 50 years of dental excellence and is awarded as the best Dental Tourism Provider in India.
