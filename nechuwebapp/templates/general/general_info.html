About Us

Nechupadam dental clinic was initially established as a diminutive family-owned dental practice, where Dr. K. T. Paulose, accompanied by his progenies, Dr. Thomas K. Paulose and Dr. Varghese K. Paulose, strived to offer top-notch dental care. Dr. K. T. Paulose, the founder and chairman, possesses a wealth of experience as a distinguished orthodontist and oral prosthetic esthetic specialist, with his offspring, alongside their respective spouses, being accomplished dental practitioners.

Over time, the clinic has evolved into a sophisticated, globally accredited dental facility that caters to the needs of patients in Kolenchery and Cochin city. Dr. Thomas K. Paulose Nechupadam, the eldest son, holds the position of CEO, while also exhibiting immense proficiency in the fields of appearance, orthodontics, dentofacial orthopedics, and implantology. Similarly, his younger brother, Dr. Varghese K. Paulose, is an accomplished orthodontist and implant specialist, renowned for his articulate oration in the realm of esthetic dentistry. The upcoming generation of dental surgeons is primed to guide the clinics towards unprecedented triumph.

Our Mission

Our paramount objective is to render dental care of unparalleled quality, adhering to global standards. We strive to promote healthy oral hygiene and preserve natural structures through minimally invasive techniques, all while utilizing state-of-the-art equipment.

Our Vision

The founding chairman of our organization, Dr. K. T. Paulose, has always envisioned being at the forefront of delivering exceptional dental services to all individuals, regardless of their social standing. His fervent desire was to provide world-class dental treatment that was economically accessible to the masses. In 1990, his aspiration of providing complimentary dental services to the underprivileged in his hometown materialized, thereby bringing his cherished dream to fruition.
